My Process

02

Conceptualization

After the discovery phase is finished, I create strategy document that fits your brands unique voice, ideal client, brand words and style.

I use the strategy as a reference to start working on concept development. After creating multiple concept options, our client picks the strongest one, and we move forward in that direction.

01

Discovery

The discovery process starts with an initial meeting that can be in person or over the phone. During this meeting I learn about the clients company and talk them through my work process. Afterwards, the client fills a brand questionnaire to help me learn more about their company and design needs.

the lynism gives a branding presentation to the client in order to discover the brand personality, values and essence of the brand. This is a collaborative work done alongside the client. Based on the personality defined previously, our team starts the research and discovery phase, looking for graphic benchmarks and learning more about the industry.

03

Creation

Once a concept is approved, Ill revise until its just right. Furthermore, I create the content necessary for your brand.
I create, present, and iterate until all deliverables are ready for launch.
